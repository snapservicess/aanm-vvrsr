ADMINISTRATION:

The Society was established on 31st March 1981,mainly with the efforts of Late Sri Adusumilli Aswardha Narayana Murthy and Late Sri Vallurupalli Venkata Rama Seshadri Rao with the main objective of establishing,aiding and maintaining Technical,Engineering aand general educational institutions.
In the year 1981, the Society was established the Adusumilli Aswardha Narayana Murthy and Vallurupalli Venkata Rama Seshadri Rao (AANM & VVRSR) Polytechnic with an intake of 180 students. Today, the Polytechnic has an intake of 1320 students and is the first choice of many students seeking admission in private polytechnics through CEEP.

PRESIDENT:

LATE SRI ADUSUMILLI ASWARDHA NARAYANA MURTHY.

  • His great aspiration towards the society to do good has resulted in today's Educational society in gudlavalleru.
  • His thought of innovation brought the sensational change in the educational field.
  • He became a great inspiration for today's generation.
  • His thought about starting the technical education resulted in the Top most college in AP.

FOUNDER CORRESPONDENT AND SCERETORY:

LATE SRI VALLURIPALLI VENKATA RAMA SESHADRI RAO.

  • Sri Vallurupalli Venkata Rama Seshadri Rao was born in 1932 in Gudlavalleru to Sri Nagaiah and Smt Mahalakshmi.
  • At the age of 51, he joined Sri Adusumilli Aswardha Narayan Murthy to establish the AANM & VVRSR Educational Society.
  • Mainly to bring technical education to the reach of rural population.
  • The Society established the AANM & VVRSR Polytechnic in Gudlavalleru in 1981.
  • He served the institute with such commitment and devotion, source of inspiration to staff and students of the institute.
  • That in no time, the institute came to be recognized as a premier institute of Polytechnic Education .
  • Which became the first choice of students seeking admission in private polytechnics through POLYCET in A.P.
